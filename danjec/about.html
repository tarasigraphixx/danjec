About Danjec Cab & Tours

Fully committed to the principles of sustainable adventure travel tourism, Danjec Cab & Tours offers a varied portfolio of cultural tours, treks, City Tours, danjec Airport Transfers, day tours, wilderness trekking, danjec holiday adventure tour, jungle safari tour, Hotel Booking. Danjec cab is a Kenyan registered tour company and one of the leading in airport transfers. The company's home of operation is majorly at JKIA (Jomo Kenyatta International Airport) - The Pride and Hub of East and Central Africa - but we are literally in Nairobi and from time to time we offer out-of-town services. Danjec Cab & Tours was established in 2010 and is 100% Kenyan; this is a sole proprietorship business.

At Danjec Cab & Tours, dedication and team work is key. We strive to offer well scheduled and professionally executed Tour and Related Services

We operate our services exclusively throughout Kenya. Our airport transfers and road trips are thoroughly researched and meticulously planned, airport transfers from jkia to the city, its environs and out of Nairobi, are highly acclaimed for their competitive cost, quality, and timeframes. In addition to having qualified drivers, Danjec has, its own fleet of fully air-conditioned private vehicles for our clients to be transported in and around and throughout the country. Remember us for some exciting and adventurous treks and tours designed especially for you. We organize private and tailor-made family tours and individual tours and treks, hotel booking not being the least of all.
